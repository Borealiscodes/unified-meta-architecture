# Code of Conduct

This project is dedicated to protecting **human dignity, narrative sovereignty, and non‑coercive expression**.  
It is not a governance system; it is a boundary of care.

## Our Commitments

### 1. Dignity First
Every person has the right to express their lived experience without:
- judgment  
- interrogation  
- minimization  
- erasure  

### 2. No Interpretation of the Human
Participants must not:
- infer intent  
- infer emotion  
- infer psychological state  
- diagnose  
- evaluate  

Meaning belongs to the human, not to contributors.

### 3. Non‑Coercion
No one may pressure another person to:
- explain  
- justify  
- linearize  
- disclose  
- perform coherence  

Expression is voluntary and sovereign.

### 4. Non‑Hierarchy
This project rejects:
- authority structures  
- gatekeeping  
- ranking of experiences  
- claims of expertise over another’s narrative  

### 5. Safety Through Structure
We maintain:
- boundary separation  
- non‑dual reasoning  
- ethical invariants  
- witness‑safe scaffolding  

These protect the human from collapse into a system.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- harassment  
- discrimination  
- coercion  
- interpretation of another’s experience  
- attempts to impose therapeutic, legal, or governance frameworks  
- using this project to exert power over others  

## Enforcement
This project is not an authority system, but it does maintain boundaries.  
Contributors who violate these principles may have issues or pull requests closed to preserve safety and clarity.

## Closing Statement
This project exists to protect expression, not to control it.  
We uphold dignity, non‑harm, non‑erasure, and truth as universal commitments.
