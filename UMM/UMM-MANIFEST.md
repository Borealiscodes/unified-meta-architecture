🌤 UMM Manifest (Minimum Viable System Version)
(This is the core identity document of UMM — the smallest version that is still complete.)
1. Identity
UMM is the user‑facing interface layer of the Reflective Ecology — a non‑coercive, multi‑plane system for navigating internal cognitive weather. It defines the world UMA operates within, the ontology UMA interprets, and the boundaries UMA must obey.
UMM is not a diagnostic tool, a therapeutic engine, or a prescriptive system. It is a container, not an interpreter; a world, not a model; a reflective interface, not an authority.
2. Purpose
UMM exists to provide a gentle, structured interface for interacting with the reflective ecology — the internal field where cognition, tone, perturbation, and nascence arise.
Its purpose is to:
make internal states legible without forcing interpretation
provide safe, low‑pressure scaffolding for reflection
support agency, pacing, and non‑coercive movement
translate complex internal weather into navigable patterns
serve as the user‑facing layer of UMA and the broader system
UMM’s purpose is reflective navigation, not inference or prediction.

UMM covers:
the user interface

the visual and structural language of the system
UMM does not include:
deep inference

behavioral manipulation
engine‑level computation (this belongs to UMA)
UMM is the surface layer — the part the user touches.

meaning sovereignty

5. Boundaries
UMM is bounded by:
the reflective primitives
