## **2.1 Purpose**

v1.2 begins a new additive cycle following the completion and archival of v1.1.  
Its purpose is to:

- extend UMA without modifying v1.0  
- build on the stabilized primitives from v1.1  
- introduce new structure only where justified  
- maintain all boundaries, membranes, and recursion ceilings  
- operate entirely within the **Archive Placement** and **Scaffold Release** logic  

v1.2 is not a revision of v1.1.  
It is a new plane.

---

## **2.2 Rules for v1.2 Development**

### **2.2.1 Additive Only**  
All contributions must add new structure.  
No retroactive modification of v1.0 or v1.1 is permitted.

### **2.2.2 Non‑Retroactive**  
v1.1 remains archived and cannot be reopened.  
v1.0 remains canonical and cannot be rewritten.

### **2.2.3 Structural Justification Required**  
Every addition must include a short justification describing:

- why the structure belongs  
- what architectural gap it resolves  
- how it aligns with UMA’s non‑coercive posture  

### **2.2.4 Boundary Respect**  
All work must respect:

- the Ethical Floor  
- the Boundary Layer  
- the Regulation Layer  
- the non‑authority stance  
- the stateless nature of UMA/UMM
