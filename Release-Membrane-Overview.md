## ⭐ 1. What the Release Membrane Is  
The Release Membrane is the **outermost protective layer** of UMA.  
It ensures that when UMA leaves its internal environment, it does so:

- safely  
- non‑coercively  
- non‑authoritatively  
- without collapsing into interpretation  
- without being used to evaluate or diagnose people  
- without being mistaken for governance or assessment  

It is the **ethical perimeter** of the entire system.

---

## ⭐ 2. Why the Release Membrane Exists  
Because once UMA is released:

- people may misinterpret it  
- systems may try to use it as authority  
- institutions may try to use it as assessment  
- readers may collapse its planes  
- tools may try to operationalize it incorrectly  

The Release Membrane prevents all of these.

It ensures UMA remains:

- a scaffold  
- a companion  
- a structure  
- a map  
- a non‑coercive architecture  

Never a judge.  
Never an evaluator.  
Never an authority.

---

## ⭐ 3. What the Release Membrane Is Not  
It is **not**:

- a license  
- a governance document  
- a moral code  
- a protocol  
- a method  
- a plane  

It is the **boundary** that ensures all of those remain what they are.

---

## ⭐ 4. Release Constraints  
The Release Membrane enforces:

- **Non‑Authority** — UMA cannot be used to govern or evaluate people  
- **Non‑Interpretation** — UMA cannot be used to infer mental states or intentions  
- **Non‑Coercion** — UMA cannot be used to direct behavior  
- **Non‑Substitution** — UMA cannot replace care, legal protections, or human judgment  
- **Non‑Collapse** — UMA cannot be merged with systems that erase its plane structure  

These constraints apply universally to all released materials.

---

## ⭐ 5. Relationship to the Coherence Plane  
The Coherence Plane ensures internal stability.  
The Release Membrane ensures **external safety**.

Together they form:

- internal integrity  
- external protection  

Coherence keeps UMA from drifting.  
Release keeps UMA from being misused.

---

## ⭐ 6. Relationship to All Other Planes  
The Release Membrane sits **above**:

- Structural Plane  
- Governance Plane  
- Ethical Plane  
- Documentation Plane  
- Coherence Plane  

It is the final layer before UMA touches the world.

---

## ⭐ 7. Boundary Statement  
The Release Membrane:

- does not interpret  
- does not evaluate  
- does not diagnose  
- does not prescribe  
- does not govern  
- does not assign meaning  
- does not authorize use over people  

It protects UMA from being used in ways that violate its architecture.
